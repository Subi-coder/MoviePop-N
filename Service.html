Servies

Online Services

  1. Ticket Booking: Seamless booking of movie tickets through the website or app.
  2. Seat Selection: Ability to choose preferred seats in the theater.
  3. Payment Options: Various payment methods including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers.
  4. Mobile Ticketing: Digital tickets that can be scanned directly from a smartphone.
  5. User Reviews and Ratings: Access to reviews and ratings of movies from other users.
  6. Notifications and Alerts: Updates on new releases, special screenings, and promotional offers.
  7. Cancellation and Refund Policies: Options for canceling or modifying bookings with clear policies.

Offline Services

  1. Box Office Sales: Ticket purchasing directly at the theater's box office.
  2. Customer Support: In-person assistance for ticket queries and issues.
  3. Gift Cards: Availability of gift cards for purchase, usable for ticket booking.
  4. Group Bookings: Assistance with booking tickets for large groups or special events.
  5. Merchandise Sales: Physical locations may sell movie-related merchandise and concessions.
  6. Special Events: Information and ticketing for film festivals, premieres, and special screenings.
  7. Accessibility Services: Assistance with accessible seating and other needs at the theater.
  8. Promotional Events: Opportunities to attend exclusive screenings or meet-and-greet events.
