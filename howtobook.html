How to Book?

1. Open the Movie Pop'n Website through Web Browser.

2. Pick your favourite movie from the lists and Genres.

3. Click on the movie thumbnail or poster.

4. A new window (called - "Book Tickets")appears and click on "Get Tickets".

5. Select the number of tickets you want and select your favourite spot in Theatre.

6. Click on submit and complete the transaction via Online Payments(UPI or Net Banking or Credit Card,etc.).

7. You will receive a message about your ticket confrimation and you can enjoy the show.
