COVID-19 Safety Protocols from Movie Pop'n

At Movie Pop'n, the health and safety of our customers and staff is our top priority. In response to the COVID-19 , we have implemented the following safety measures and regulations to ensure a safe movie-going experience:

Health and Safety Measures

  1. Contactless Transactions: We encourage online bookings and contactless payment options to minimize physical interactions at the cinema.
  2. Capacity Limits: Cinemas may operate at reduced capacity to ensure social distancing. Please check the specific venue guidelines when booking your tickets.
  3. Mask Requirements: Guests are encouraged to wear masks in accordance with local health regulations and cinema policies. Please check the specific requirements for each venue.
  4. Enhanced Cleaning Protocols: Cinemas will conduct regular cleaning and sanitation of high-touch surfaces and common areas to promote a safe environment.
  5. Health Screening: Some venues may conduct temperature checks or health screenings at entry points. Guests displaying symptoms of illness may be denied entry.

Refund and Rescheduling Policy

In the event of a government mandate or health regulations that require the cancellation or rescheduling of a movie, we will offer full refunds or options to reschedule your tickets. If you have purchased tickets and can no longer attend due to COVID-19 related issues, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Staying Informed

We encourage you to stay informed about local health guidelines and regulations, as these may change based on evolving circumstances. Visit Local Health Department for the latest updates.
